Frequently Asked Questions

What types of security services do you offer?

CentralGuards offers a comprehensive range of security services including physical security (guards, patrols), electronic security (surveillance, alarm systems), specialized services (executive protection, event security), and security consulting. Each service can be customized to meet your specific requirements.

How quickly can you deploy security personnel?

For standard security deployments, we typically require 24-48 hours notice. However, in emergency situations, we can deploy security personnel within 4-6 hours depending on your location and specific requirements. For large-scale events or specialized services, we recommend contacting us at least 1-2 weeks in advance for optimal planning.

Are your security guards licensed and insured?

Yes, all CentralGuards security personnel are fully licensed, bonded, and insured in accordance with state and federal regulations. Our company carries comprehensive general liability insurance, workers' compensation, and professional liability coverage. We're happy to provide verification of our credentials and insurance certificates upon request.

How do you handle emergency situations?

CentralGuards has established emergency response protocols for various scenarios. Our security personnel are trained in emergency procedures including first aid, CPR, and disaster response. We maintain a 24/7 command center for immediate support and coordination with local law enforcement and emergency services when needed.

Can you provide a free security assessment for my property?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial security assessments for new clients. Our security experts will evaluate your property, identify potential vulnerabilities, and recommend appropriate security measures. For more comprehensive security audits or specialized assessments, we provide detailed proposals outlining the scope and cost based on your specific needs.
