Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do I need to work as a security officer?

+

Basic qualifications include a high school diploma or equivalent, a clean criminal history, and the ability to pass a background check. Depending on the position, you may need additional certifications or licenses specific to your state. CentralGuards provides comprehensive training to qualified candidates.

What benefits does CentralGuards offer?

+

We offer a comprehensive benefits package that includes health, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) retirement plan with company match, paid time off, paid training, career advancement opportunities, employee assistance program, and uniform allowance. Specific benefits may vary by position and employment status.

How long does the hiring process take?

+

Our hiring process typically takes 2-3 weeks from application to offer, depending on the position and background check requirements. This includes application review, phone screening, interviews, assessments, and background checks. We strive to keep candidates informed throughout the process.

What training will I receive?

+

CentralGuards provides comprehensive initial training for all security positions, including security fundamentals, emergency response, conflict resolution, and site-specific protocols. We also offer ongoing professional development, specialized training for specific roles, and support for industry certifications.

Are there opportunities for advancement?

+

Yes, we strongly believe in promoting from within. Many of our supervisors and managers started as security officers. We offer clear career paths, leadership development programs, and tuition assistance to help employees advance their careers within CentralGuards.

What if I don't see a job that matches my skills?

+

You can join our talent network to receive notifications when relevant positions become available. You can also submit a general application for future consideration. We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team.
