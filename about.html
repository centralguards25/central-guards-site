About CentralGuards

Your trusted security partner for over two decades, providing comprehensive security solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Our Company

Founded in 2001, CentralGuards has grown to become one of the leading security service providers in the industry. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has allowed us to develop comprehensive security solutions that address the evolving challenges faced by businesses today.

At CentralGuards, we believe that security is not just about protection; it's about creating peace of mind. Our team of highly trained professionals works tirelessly to ensure that our clients can focus on their core business operations, knowing that their security needs are in capable hands.

With a nationwide presence and a global outlook, we provide tailored security solutions to clients across various industries, including finance, healthcare, retail, and government sectors.

Our History

2001

Founded

2005

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to cover the entire East Coast with over 500 security professionals.

2010

Technology Integration

Launched our integrated security technology division, adding electronic security to our services.

2015

National Recognition

Named "Security Provider of the Year" and expanded to nationwide operations.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched CentralGuards Connect, our proprietary digital security management platform.

Today

Industry Leader

Now serving over 2,000 clients nationwide with comprehensive security solutions.
